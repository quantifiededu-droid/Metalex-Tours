About Metalex Tours

Metalex Tours is a premier travel agency based in Mpraeso, in the heart of Ghana's Eastern Region. We specialize in creating unforgettable experiences, bridging the gap between local heritage and modern travel convenience.

Whether you are looking for a weekend getaway in the Kwahu hills, specialized visa assistance, or international flight bookings, our dedicated team ensures every detail of your journey is handled with professionalism and care.

  • ✔ Local Expertise: We know the hidden gems of Mpraeso and beyond.
  • ✔ Integrity: Transparent pricing and honest travel consultation.
  • ✔ Availability: Open 7 days a week from dawn to late evening.
  • ✔ Personal Touch: Every traveler is treated like family.
